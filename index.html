A Northeast Ohio Family Business Built on Quality

Westfield Cabinet Co. is a family-owned RTA cabinet supplier based in Strongsville, Ohio, serving Cleveland and the surrounding area. We started this business on a simple idea: homeowners and contractors shouldn't have to choose between quality construction and a fair price.

Every cabinet we sell is built to the same standard — solid plywood boxes, dovetail-joined drawers, and soft-close hardware — because that's what a cabinet should be, not just what the high-end brands charge extra for.
